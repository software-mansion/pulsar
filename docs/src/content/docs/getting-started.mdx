### Hello! 👋

Haptics isn't just about phone vibration. Haptics convey **feelings** and **emotions**. Proper haptics in your app can increase immersion and significantly improve the user experience. Your app can transform from a regular app into a **premium experience** for your users.  
Your app deserves to be ✨**special**✨ !

We know that designing custom haptic patterns is time-consuming and not easy. That's why Pulsar is a platform that helps your app provide the best user experience by using the **right** haptics. So if you want to find the perfect one, visit our interactive playground.
