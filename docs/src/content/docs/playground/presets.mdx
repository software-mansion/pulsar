## Device Connection
<Connection client:load>
<Steps>
1. Download the **PulsarApp** for App Store or Play Store.

    <div>
    <div style={{display: 'flex', alignItems: 'center'}}>
      <img src='/interactive-playground/appIcon.png' width={70} style={{marginRight: '10px'}} />
      <div>
       **PulsarApp**
      </div>
    </div>
    <div style={{display: 'flex', marginTop: '20px'}}>
      <img src='/interactive-playground/apple.svg' height={50} style={{marginRight: '10px'}} />
      <img src="/interactive-playground/google.png" height={50} />
    </div>
   </div>
   <div className='connection-spaceVertical' />

2. Open Playground and find [Device Connection](/playground/presets#device-connection) section.
   <img src='/interactive-playground/code_red.png' style={{maxWidth: '400px', width: '100%'}} />
   <div className='connection-spaceVertical' />

3. Type **Paring code** into **PulsarApp** and click **Connect** button.
   <img src='/interactive-playground/connection.gif' style={{maxWidth: '220px', width: '100%'}} />
   <div className='connection-spaceVertical' />

4. Check [Device Connection](/playground/presets#device-connection) section if your device is successfully connected.
   <img src='/interactive-playground/code_green.png' style={{maxWidth: '400px', width: '100%'}} />
   <div className='connection-spaceVertical' />

5. Fund your preset and click button with phone to play them on the device.
   <img src='/interactive-playground/play.gif' style={{maxWidth: '300px', width: '100%'}} />
   <div className='connection-spaceVertical' />

6. Congratulation! 🎉 🥳
</Steps>
</Connection>
