# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Pulsar app ("App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

## Summary

Pulsar does not require user accounts or login. We do not intentionally collect personal or sensitive data. The App collects limited, anonymized usage analytics and technical diagnostics through third-party services to help us improve stability and user experience.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Usage Analytics

We use **PostHog** to collect anonymized usage data, including:

- Screens visited and navigation flow
- In-app actions (e.g., connecting a device, applying filters, using the playground)
- App lifecycle events (app installed, updated, opened, sent to background)
- Touch interactions with UI elements

This data does not include your name, email, or any personally identifiable information. It is used solely to understand how users interact with the App and to improve its features.

### 1.2 Crash and Error Reports

We use **Sentry** to automatically collect crash reports and error diagnostics, including:

- Error messages and stack traces
- Device type, operating system version, and app version
- Performance traces and navigation events at the time of a crash

This information is used exclusively to identify and fix bugs. It does not include personally identifiable information.

### 1.3 Deep Linking

We use **Detour** (by Software Mansion) to handle deep links that allow the App to connect to your device via a URL. Detour may process link metadata (such as whether a link was opened and from what context) to support this functionality. Detour is designed with privacy-aware practices and does not collect personal information.

### 1.4 Over-the-Air Updates

The App uses **Expo Updates** to deliver app updates automatically. When checking for updates, the App communicates with Expo's servers. This process may involve standard network metadata (e.g., IP address) but does not involve personal data.

### 1.5 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device using secure on-device storage:

- **Connection token** — a temporary identifier used to re-establish a connection to your device without re-scanning. This token is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
- **App review prompt state** — a flag indicating whether you have been asked to review the App. This contains no personal data.

---

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

- Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact information
- Precise or approximate location data
- Camera, microphone, or media content
- Contacts, calendar, or health data
- Advertising identifiers
- Financial or payment information

## 4. How We Use the Information

The limited data we collect is used to:

- Diagnose and fix crashes and technical issues
- Understand which features are used and improve them
- Deliver app updates and bug fixes
- Enable device connection via deep links

We do not sell, rent, or share any collected data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Analytics data** is retained by PostHog in accordance with their data retention policy.
- **Crash reports** are retained by Sentry in accordance with their data retention policy.
- **Locally stored data** (connection token, review prompt flag) remains on your device until the App is uninstalled or you clear the App's data.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
